The ULG Method

How to Study

  • Your course has ~28 modules (varies by state). Move through them efficiently — the modules unlock what actually matters.
  • The simulated exams are your real studying. There are 3–4 of them. Take them over and over — they are the only study tool you need.
  • The bar: consistently passing EVERY simulated exam at 90%+.
  • Don't memorize answers. Every question you miss, look it up and understand WHY the right answer is right.

If you can consistently pass at 90%, you will pass your state exam.
