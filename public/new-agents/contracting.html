Get Contracted

You passed. Now let's make it official — your license, your NPN, and your carriers. Follow every step in order.

Phase 1

Buy Your State License

  1. Go to NIPR

    Click Start Now.

    Apply for Your License →

  2. Apply as an Individual

    Identify with your SSN. Enter your last name, SSN, and date of birth, then click Start.

  3. Application Settings

    Product type Producer Licensing
    Application type Initial
    Residency type Resident

  4. Select Your State & Purchase

    Click next, search for your resident state — the one you just passed in — and purchase your license.

  5. Download the NIPR App

    Get it on your phone and sign in.

Phase 2 24–48 hours later

Find Your NPN

Your NPN appears 24–48 hours after you pass. Don't panic if it's not instant.

Phase 3

Request Your Carriers

After you send your info, your upline will invite you as an agent. Watch your email — your onboarding link is coming. Then follow this exactly.

Start in NLC

Open the onboarding link from your email and log in to Gateway. Go to NLC and start the onboarding.

Create a Google Doc with three things: your bank name, routing number, and account number — this is the account your direct deposits and carrier commissions will hit. Upload that doc.

  • Bank name
  • Routing number
  • Account number

NLC Will Ask for E&O — Get It Through SureLC

NLC's onboarding will ask you to upload E&O insurance. You'll buy it through SureLC — but SureLC makes you complete its own onboarding before it lets you purchase. Register for a SureLC account, then work through it.

Register for SureLC →
  • Doing business as: INDIVIDUAL
  • Upload your bank info (the same Google Doc as before).
  • Answer the FINRA questions.

AML Training, Then Buy Your E&O

In SureLC

Before SureLC lets you buy E&O, complete the AML requirement: left menu → CE & Training → Anti-Money Laundering Training → Start Training (through SuranceBay, LLC). Finish it and download your completion certificate.

Now buy the E&O: left menu → E&O Insurance → BUY PLAN A → sign → download your E&O certificate.

You leave SureLC with two documents in hand: your AML certificate and your E&O certificate.

Back to NLC to Finish

Upload your E&O information and your AML training certificate. Answer the questions as you go — on question 12, select: Individual Agent

Then find the blue + button near the top left → NEW CONTRACTING → request every single carrier one by one.

Request every carrier EXCEPT Occidental. Do not request Occidental.

Go through everything thoroughly and sign every document — a carrier isn't fully requested until it's signed.
